ZionBoard helps you build beautiful bulletins quickly. Because it isn't connected to any ward or stake database, you can start right away and keep using the same link as long as you like. Here are a few of the things you can do:

  • Create digital bulletins in your browser
  • Print PDFs for members who prefer paper
  • Share a public link or QR code with your ward—the QR code is yours forever
  • Store multiple bulletins and reuse previous templates
  • Duplicate bulletins to save time on weekly updates
